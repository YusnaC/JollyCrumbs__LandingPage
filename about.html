Who We Are

- About Us -

"Have a jolly day!" That's what we always say to our customers. We are dedicated to providing you with a comfortable environment, friendly prices and friendly staff who are always ready to help with your orders. We serve a variety of dishes, such as cakes, breads, and some main dishes and drinks for you to enjoy. Even though our business is still small now, we have great desire to let everyone know that we exist and grow our business to be bigger.

Our cafe is based in Bali, Indonesia. We provide delivery throughout Indonesia using a safe delivery service, so that your order will arrive at your location in good condition. For areas outside the city, reservations can be made by calling the number listed in the contact section, so that we can process your order as soon as possible. Our cafe has also been registered on various platforms such as ShopeeFood, GrabFood and also GoFood, so it can make it easier for you to order.
